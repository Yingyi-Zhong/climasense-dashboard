HOW IT WORKS

Transforming Weather Data into Daily Decisions

Our system turns complex weather data into simple and actionable insights, helping Los Angeles County residents plan their day smarter.

The Weather Recommendation System integrates data from the Open-Meteo Weather Forecast and Air Quality APIs, combining temperature, humidity, wind speed, and comfort index with a custom rule-based engine.

Each day, it automatically generates personalized activity and outfit suggestions for more than 80 cities across Los Angeles County, visualized through an interactive dashboard.

By combining data accuracy, behavioral logic, and clear visualization, the project connects environmental data with everyday comfort and helps users make confident daily decisions.
